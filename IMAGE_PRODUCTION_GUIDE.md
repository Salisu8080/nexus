# IMAGE PRODUCTION GUIDE
## 30-Day Facebook Content Calendar - Nexus College of Nursing Sciences

**Purpose:** This guide provides detailed visual specifications for each day's content, ensuring consistent branding and professional presentation across all Facebook posts.

---

## BRAND VISUAL GUIDELINES

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- **Royal Blue:** #2B3B94 (Main brand color)
- **Deep Blue:** #1e3c72 (Secondary brand color)
- **Gold/Yellow:** #FDB913 (Accent color)

**Supporting Colors:**
- White: #FFFFFF
- Light Gray: #F8F9FA
- Dark Gray: #333333
- Red (CTA): #DC3545 or #B9272D
- Green (Success): #28A745

### Typography Guidelines
- **Headers:** Bold, sans-serif fonts (Montserrat, Poppins, or Roboto)
- **Body Text:** Clean, readable fonts (Open Sans, Lato)
- **Size Hierarchy:** Maintain clear visual hierarchy with varying font sizes

### Logo Usage
- Institution logo should appear on branded posts
- Placement: Top-left or bottom-right corner
- Maintain clear space around logo
- Never distort or alter logo colors

### Image Dimensions
- **Standard Facebook Post:** 1080x1080px (Square - optimal for feed)
- **Link Share Post:** 1200x630px (Landscape)
- **Story Format (if used):** 1080x1920px (Vertical)

## GENERAL DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Photo Quality Standards
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1080px on shortest side
- **Format:** JPEG or PNG
- **File Size:** Under 1MB for optimal Facebook loading
- **Lighting:** Bright, natural light preferred
- **Composition:** Follow rule of thirds
- **Focus:** Sharp, in-focus imagery

### Text Overlay Guidelines
- **Maximum Text:** 20% of image area (Facebook best practice)
- **Font Size:** Minimum 24pt for readability on mobile
- **Contrast:** Ensure text is readable (use overlays if needed)
- **Spacing:** Adequate padding around text
- **Alignment:** Consistent across all posts

### Branding Consistency
- **Logo Placement:** Consistent position (recommend bottom-right)
- **Logo Size:** 10-15% of image size
- **Colors:** Stick to brand color palette
- **Fonts:** Use maximum 2-3 font families
- **Style:** Maintain consistent design language

### Photography Requirements
**Essential Original Photos Needed:**
1. Practical Laboratory (multiple angles)
2. Practical Learning Centre (multiple angles)
3. Library interior
4. CBT Center
5. Hostel/accommodation facilities
6. School bus/transportation
7. College clinic facilities
8. Campus exterior/entrance
9. Students in classroom (with consent)
10. General campus life scenes

**Optional but Recommended:**
- Staff/faculty photos (with permission)
- Graduate success photos (with permission)
- Student testimonial photos (with consent)

### Step 1: Gather Assets (Week 1)
- Photograph all facilities
- Collect staff photos (with permissions)

- Organize in folders by day number

### Step 2: Design Creation (Week 2)
- Design Days 1-15
- Review and refine
- Ensure brand consistency

### Step 3: Design Creation Continued (Week 3)
- Design Days 16-30
- Review all designs together
- Make adjustments for cohesion

### Step 4: Quality Check (Week 4)
- Review all 30 designs
- Check dimensions and file sizes

- Spell-check all text
- Verify brand consistency

### Step 5: Scheduling (Ongoing)
- Upload to Facebook scheduling tool

## FILE NAMING CONVENTION
Format: `NexusCNS_Day[XX]_[Description].jpg`

Examples:
- `NexusCNS_Day01_Welcome.jpg`
- `NexusCNS_Day03_Laboratory.jpg`

---

## ACCESSIBILITY CONSIDERATIONS
- **Alt Text:** Provide descriptive alt text for each image
- **Contrast Ratio:** Maintain minimum 4.5:1 for text readability
- **Text-Free Versions:** Keep important info in caption, not just image
- **Color Blind Friendly:** Don't rely solely on color to convey meaning

---

## ENGAGEMENT OPTIMIZATION
- **First 3 Seconds:** Most important visual elements should be immediately visible
- **Mobile-First:** Design for mobile viewing (70%+ of Facebook users)
- **Thumb-Stopping:** Use bold visuals that stand out in crowded feeds
- **Emotional Connection:** Include human elements (people, emotions) when possible

---
