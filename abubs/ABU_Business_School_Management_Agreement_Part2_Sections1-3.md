## 2. PURPOSE OF AGREEMENT

### 2.1 Background

WHEREAS, Saypeace AI Technologies has successfully developed, delivered, and deployed a comprehensive dual-platform educational system for ABU Business School, consisting of:

1. **Main Website** hosted at https://businessschool.abu.edu.ng/
2. **Learning Management System (LMS)** hosted at https://businessschool.abu.edu.ng/lms

### 2.2 Purpose

The purpose of this Agreement is to establish a formal, legally binding framework for the provision of ongoing management, maintenance, technical support, content management, security, hosting, and enhancement services for both the Main Website and Learning Management System.

### 2.3 Objectives

This Agreement aims to ensure:

- **Continuous Availability**: Maintain optimal uptime and system performance
- **Security**: Protect institutional data and user information
- **Content Freshness**: Regular updates to website content and LMS materials
- **Technical Excellence**: Proactive maintenance and timely issue resolution
- **Scalability**: Support institutional growth and evolving needs
- **Compliance**: Adherence to data protection and security standards
- **User Satisfaction**: Excellent service delivery to students, staff, and visitors
