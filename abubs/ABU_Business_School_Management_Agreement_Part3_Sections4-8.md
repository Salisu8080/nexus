## 4. TERM OF AGREEMENT

### 4.1 Initial Term

This Agreement shall commence on the date of execution by both Parties and shall remain in full force and effect for an initial period of **FIVE (5) YEARS** from the commencement date (the "**Initial Term**").

### 4.2 Renewal

Upon expiration of the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive periods of **ONE (1) YEAR** each (each a "**Renewal Term**"), unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **NINETY (90) DAYS** prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

### 4.3 Price Adjustment Upon Renewal

**Annual Review:**
- Service fees shall be subject to annual review and adjustment
- Maximum price increase shall not exceed **10% per annum**
- Price adjustments shall be based on:
  - Market inflation rates
  - Changes in operational costs
  - Industry standard pricing
  - Scope of service modifications

**Notification:**
- Saypeace shall provide written notice of any price adjustment at least **SIXTY (60) DAYS** prior to the renewal date
- ABU Business School may reject the adjustment and exercise its non-renewal rights within thirty (30) days of receiving such notice

### 4.4 Early Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement prior to the expiration of the then-current term by providing **NINETY (90) DAYS** written notice to the other Party, subject to:

1. Payment of all outstanding fees up to the termination date
2. Payment of an early termination fee equivalent to **THREE (3) MONTHS** of the monthly service fees
3. Cooperation in transition and handover processes
4. Return or destruction of confidential information

The early termination fee shall be waived in cases of:
- Material breach by the other Party (not cured within 30 days)
- Force majeure lasting longer than 90 consecutive days
- Mutual written agreement of both Parties

## 5. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)

### 5.1 System Uptime Guarantee

**Uptime Commitment:**
- Saypeace guarantees **99.5% system uptime** for both the Website and LMS
- Uptime calculation excludes scheduled maintenance windows
- Measurement period: Calendar month

**Scheduled Maintenance:**
- Maintenance Window: **Last Sunday of each month, 2:00 AM - 6:00 AM WAT**
- Emergency maintenance: As needed with advance notice when possible
- Major upgrades: Scheduled during low-traffic periods with 7-day advance notice

**Uptime Calculation:**
```
Monthly Uptime % = [(Total Minutes in Month - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes in Month] × 100
```

**Service Credits for Uptime Failures:**
- 99.0% - 99.4% uptime: 10% credit of monthly fee
- 98.0% - 98.9% uptime: 25% credit of monthly fee
- Below 98.0% uptime: 50% credit of monthly fee

Service credits are the exclusive remedy for uptime failures.

### 5.2 Support Response Times

**Issue Priority Levels:**

**Critical (Priority 1):**
- Definition: Complete system outage, security breach, data loss
- Response Time: **4 hours**
- Resolution Target: **24 hours**
- Escalation: Immediate notification to senior management

**High Priority (Priority 2):**
- Definition: Major functionality broken, affecting multiple users
- Response Time: **12 hours** (business hours)
- Resolution Target: **48 hours**
- Examples: LMS enrollment failures, payment processing issues

**Medium Priority (Priority 3):**
- Definition: Minor functionality issues, affecting limited users
- Response Time: **24 hours** (business hours)
- Resolution Target: **5 business days**
- Examples: Formatting issues, minor bugs

**Low Priority (Priority 4):**
- Definition: Cosmetic issues, feature requests, general inquiries
- Response Time: **72 hours** (business hours)
- Resolution Target: **10 business days**
- Examples: UI tweaks, documentation requests

**Business Hours:** Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM WAT (excluding public holidays)

### 5.3 Content Management Turnaround Times

**Standard Content Updates:**
- News articles, blog posts: **2-3 business days** from receipt of properly formatted content
- Faculty profiles: **3 business days**
- Event listings: **1-2 business days**
- Homepage banners/sliders: **2 business days**

**LMS Content Updates:**
- Course material uploads (up to 10GB): **3-5 business days**
- Exam question uploads (up to 100 questions): **3 business days**
- Course catalog updates: **2 business days**

**Expedited Service:**
- Rush requests: **24-hour turnaround** (subject to 50% surcharge)
- Emergency updates: **Same business day** (subject to 100% surcharge)

### 5.4 Backup and Recovery

**Backup Schedule:**
- **Daily automated backups**: 2:00 AM WAT
- **Weekly manual verification**: Every Sunday
- **Monthly full system backup**: First day of each month

**Backup Retention:**
- Daily backups: Retained for **30 days**
- Weekly backups: Retained for **90 days**
- Monthly backups: Retained for **1 year**

**Recovery Time Objectives (RTO):**
- Critical data: **4 hours**
- Complete system restoration: **24 hours**
- Historical data: **48 hours**

**Recovery Point Objectives (RPO):**
- Maximum data loss: **24 hours** (one day's worth of data)

### 5.5 Reporting Schedule

**Monthly Reports:**
- Delivered by the **5th business day** of each month
- Cover previous month's activities and metrics

**Quarterly Reports:**
- Comprehensive system health analysis
- Delivered within **10 business days** of quarter end

**Annual Reports:**
- Full year performance review
- Strategic recommendations
- Delivered by **January 31st** for preceding year
