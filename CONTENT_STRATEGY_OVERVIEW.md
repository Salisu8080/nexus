# CONTENT STRATEGY OVERVIEW
## 30-Day Facebook Campaign - Nexus College of Nursing Sciences

**Campaign Goal:** Drive applications, build brand awareness, and establish Nexus CNS as the premier nursing education institution in Northern Nigeria

**Target Region:** Katsina State and Northern Nigeria
**Duration:** 30 Days (Repeatable Campaign)
**Platform:** Facebook (Primary)
**Campaign Type:** Educational Marketing & Lead Generation

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This 30-day content strategy is designed to transform Nexus College of Nursing Sciences' Facebook presence from a passive information source into an active recruitment and engagement engine. The strategy addresses the unique challenges of marketing nursing education in Northern Nigeria while leveraging the institution's genuine strengths: modern facilities, practical training focus, accessible education, and community impact.

The campaign follows a proven storytelling arc that:
1. Introduces the institution and builds awareness (Days 1-7)
2. Educates about nursing careers and opportunities (Days 8-14)
3. Addresses objections and builds trust (Days 15-21)
4. Creates urgency and drives action (Days 22-29)
5. Nurtures relationships for long-term engagement (Day 30)

## ENGAGEMENT TACTICS

### Daily Engagement Strategies:

#### 1. **Question-Based Engagement**
- Ask followers to comment their opinions, dreams, or concerns
- Example: "What nursing specialty interests you most?"
- **Goal:** Algorithm boost through comments + audience insights

#### 2. **Call-to-Tag**
- Encourage followers to tag friends who should see the post
- Example: "Tag someone who should become a nurse!"
- **Goal:** Organic reach expansion

#### 3. **Public Commitment**
- Ask followers to comment when they've taken action
- Example: "Comment APPLIED when you submit your form!"
- **Goal:** Social proof + psychological commitment

#### 4. **Shareability Design**
- Create quote graphics and inspirational posts designed to be shared
- **Goal:** Viral potential and reach expansion

#### 5. **Poll & Choice**
- Present options for audience to choose/vote on
- Example: "Internet or Books - what can't you study without?"
- **Goal:** Easy engagement + audience preferences data

#### 6. **Story Requests**
- Ask audience to share their own stories and experiences
- Example: "Tell us about a nurse who inspired you"
- **Goal:** User-generated content + emotional connection

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT & REPUTATION PROTECTION

### Response Protocol:

#### Positive Comments:
- **Response Time:** Within 12 hours
- **Tone:** Grateful, enthusiastic
- **Action:** Like, reply with personalized message, encourage next step

#### Questions/Inquiries:
- **Response Time:** Within 6 hours (business hours)
- **Tone:** Helpful, informative, welcoming
- **Action:** Provide answer publicly (benefits others), offer DM for detailed discussion

#### Negative Comments/Complaints:
- **Response Time:** Within 4 hours
- **Tone:** Professional, empathetic, solution-oriented
- **Action:**
  1. Acknowledge concern publicly
  2. Apologize if warranted (without admitting fault prematurely)
  3. Move conversation to private message for resolution
  4. Never delete unless abusive/spam
  5. Document and escalate to management

#### Trolling/Spam:
- **Response Time:** Immediate
- **Action:**
  1. Do not engage emotionally
  2. Report and hide/delete if abusive
  3. Block repeat offenders
  4. Never argue publicly

### Comment Moderation Guidelines:
**Allowed:**
- Genuine questions
- Constructive criticism
- Competitor mentions (respond professionally)
- Student experiences (positive or negative - address honestly)

**Remove/Hide:**
- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Spam and promotional content
- Abusive language
- Inappropriate content
- Repeated trolling

## CULTURAL SENSITIVITY GUIDELINES

### Northern Nigeria Cultural Context:

#### Islamic Considerations:
- **Timing:** Avoid major posting during Friday Jummah prayers (12-2 PM Friday)
- **Content:** Respect Islamic values in imagery and messaging
- **Language:** Incorporate respectful Islamic greetings when appropriate (e.g., "Sannu!")
- **Imagery:** Ensure modesty in all visual content

#### Gender Sensitivity:
- **Representation:** Show both male and female students/nurses
- **Messaging:** Address concerns about co-education respectfully
- **Safety:** Emphasize safe, respectful environment
- **Opportunities:** Highlight that nursing is for everyone, regardless of gender

#### Language Considerations:
- **Primary:** English (clear, simple)
- **Secondary:** Occasional Hausa greetings for local connection (e.g., "Sannu", "Nagode")
- **Avoid:** Overly Western slang or culturally inappropriate expressions
- **Clarity:** Prefer simple language over complex medical jargon

#### Visual Representation:
- **Diversity:** Show diverse student body
- **Modesty:** Ensure appropriate dress in all imagery
- **Local Context:** Use imagery that resonates with Northern Nigerian audience
- **Authentic:** Prefer local/authentic imagery over overly Western stock photos

## RISK MITIGATION

### Potential Challenges & Solutions:

#### Challenge 1: Low Engagement
**Mitigation:**
- A/B test posting times
- Increase question-based engagement
- Use Facebook ads to boost key posts
- Analyze and adapt content based on what performs

#### Challenge 2: Negative Comments/Reputation Issues
**Mitigation:**
- Respond professionally and promptly
- Have crisis communication plan ready
- Never delete genuine criticism - address it
- Document and learn from feedback

#### Challenge 3: Content Fatigue (30 days is long)
**Mitigation:**
- Vary content types significantly
- Use different visual styles
- Mix educational with entertaining
- Include user-generated content breaks

#### Challenge 4: Low Conversion Despite Engagement
**Mitigation:**
- Simplify application process
- Add WhatsApp quick contact option
- Use retargeting ads for engagers who don't apply
- Offer virtual campus tour or phone consultation

#### Challenge 5: Resource/Time Constraints
**Mitigation:**
- Batch create all content in advance
- Use scheduling tools
- Have backup content ready
- Prioritize quality over perfection
